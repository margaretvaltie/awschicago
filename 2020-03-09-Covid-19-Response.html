Events will Continue as Scheduled

We are monitoring the COVID-19 news and health recommendations. Chicago is in a low-risk area, so events will proceed. We are following all public health advice as well as the World Health Organization (WHO)'s recommendations for mass gatherings. If you do not feel well, please do not attend events. Always wash your hands!

About the AWS Chicago user group

This AWS user group is a user-run, user-focused community in Chicago. We hold monthly, free events in the Loop. We rotate host offices and topics but aim for consistent format. Events include pizza and drinks up front, introductions, 2 to 3 quality non-sales presentations, then wrap with q&a.
