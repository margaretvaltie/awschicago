Update: COVID-19 Response

post date: March 11, 2020

March and April Events will be Virtual

With updates from the local Chicago government and other tech communities, we will move to virtual events in response to the novel conronavirus, or COVID-19.

Chicago remains a low-risk area but the latest public health advice from the CDC and World Health Organization (WHO) recommends "social distancing". As always, please stay home if you feel sick and wash your hands!

About the AWS Chicago user group

This AWS user group is a user-run, user-focused community in Chicago. We hold monthly, free events in the Loop. We rotate host offices and topics but aim for consistent format. Events include pizza and drinks up front, introductions, 2 to 3 quality non-sales presentations, then wrap with q&a.
